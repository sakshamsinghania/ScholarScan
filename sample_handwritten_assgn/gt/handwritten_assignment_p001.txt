CASE STUDY 1: Ransomware Attack on a Smart Hospital

1. Detection Phase

Early Indicators:
• Sudden system lockouts across hospital devices
• Patient data becomes inaccessible
• Ransom message appears ("Pay 50 Bitcoin")
• Malfunctioning IoT devices (ventilators, monitors)
• Unusual network traffic or system slowdown

Logs to Check:
Network Logs:
• Suspicious outgoing connections
• Unknown IP communication

Device Logs:
• Unauthorized access attempts
• Firmware irregularities
